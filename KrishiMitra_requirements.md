
# KrishiMitra AI – Advanced Requirements Document

## 1. Executive Summary

KrishiMitra AI is a multilingual, voice-first AI assistant designed to empower farmers with real-time market intelligence, government scheme awareness, and smart agricultural advisory. 

The system aims to increase farmer income, reduce middlemen dependency, and improve decision-making using AI-driven recommendations.

---

## 2. Problem Definition

Farmers face:
- Lack of real-time mandi (market) price visibility
- Limited awareness of government subsidies and schemes
- Exploitation by intermediaries
- Inadequate access to localized crop advisory
- Digital literacy and language barriers
- Low-bandwidth internet challenges

Impact:
- Reduced profit margins
- Poor crop planning decisions
- Missed government benefits

---

## 3. Product Vision

To build an AI-powered digital farming companion that ensures every farmer has access to market intelligence, government benefits, and smart advisory — regardless of connectivity or literacy level.

---

## 4. Stakeholders

- Small & marginal farmers
- Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
- Government agriculture departments
- Agricultural NGOs
- Rural cooperatives

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

### 5.1 User Interaction Layer
- Voice input (speech-to-text)
- Text-based queries
- SMS-based low-bandwidth interaction
- Multilingual interface (Hindi + regional languages)

### 5.2 Market Intelligence Module
- Real-time mandi price tracking
- AI-based best market recommendation
- Historical price trend visualization
- Crop price forecasting (future enhancement)

### 5.3 Government Scheme Module
- Scheme eligibility checker
- Subsidy information guidance
- Crop insurance awareness
- Direct scheme application guidance links

### 5.4 Agricultural Advisory Module
- Weather-based crop alerts
- Basic pest & disease awareness
- Preventive farming practices guidance

### 5.5 AI Intelligence Layer
- NLP-based intent recognition
- Context-aware crop advisory
- Price comparison algorithm
- Smart recommendation engine

### 5.6 Notification & Alert System
- Price spike alerts
- Weather alerts
- Scheme deadline reminders

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- Response time under 3 seconds
- Operates in 2G/low-bandwidth conditions
- 99% uptime
- Scalable to 1M+ users
- Secure encrypted communication (HTTPS)
- Data privacy compliant

---

## 7. Constraints

- Must use verified mandi and government APIs
- Must support low-end Android devices
- Must not provide financial guarantees or false forecasts

---

## 8. KPIs & Success Metrics

- Increase in average farmer selling price
- Reduction in middlemen dependency
- Active monthly users (MAU)
- Scheme enrollment increase rate
- User satisfaction score

---

## 9. Future Roadmap

- AI-driven crop yield prediction
- Demand forecasting engine
- Direct buyer-farmer marketplace integration
